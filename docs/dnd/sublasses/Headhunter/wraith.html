Wraith

Level 3 — Flicker Strike

When you hit your Target with your Signature Weapon, you may immediately attempt to Hide as part of the same action (no bonus action required).

You don't need full cover — half cover, dim light, or a brief obstruction is enough.

This can be used to instantly use Evanesce and will hide you regardless of obstruction.

Level 3 — Intangible

Your Target has disadvantage on any Wisdom (Perception) checks made to detect you.

Level 10 — Fear Echo

When you deal Killshot damage to your Target, you can force it to make a Wisdom saving throw (DC = 8 + PB + Dex or Cha mod).

On a failure, until the end of your next turn:

  • It is Frightened of you
  • Its speed is halved
  • And it cannot take reactions

On a success, it still cannot take reactions.

Level 18 — Veilbreaker

When you are Hidden from your Target and make an attack against it:

  • The attack roll has advantage
  • It must make a Wisdom saving throw or be Dazed until the start of your next turn. (Once per Target per turn.)

When dazed you can only use one of three in your turn: action, bonus action, movement.
