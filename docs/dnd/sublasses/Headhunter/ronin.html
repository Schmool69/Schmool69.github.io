Ronin

Level 3 — Honourable Duel

As a bonus action, you can declare a Duel with your Target.

When you do, a 10-foot radius dueling zone is established around you until the start of your next turn (it moves with you).

Anyone not your target must make a Wisdom saving throw before attacking you or entering the circle (Wisdom DC 8 + PB + Cha Mod). On a fail, the attacker must pick a new target or cancel the attack.

This can be done as many times equal to your proficiency bonus - resets on long rest.

Level 10 — Piercing Shame

When you hit your Target with your Signature Weapon and deal Killshot damage, you can mark it with Shame until the end of your next turn.

A Shamed creature experiences:

  • Penalty equal to your proficiency bonus on attack rolls against any creature other than you
  • Its speed is reduced by 10 ft while moving away from you
  • Opportunity attacks you make against the Target have advantage

Level 18 — Way of the Omen

If your Target is the only enemy within 20 feet of you:

  • You have advantage on attack rolls against the Target
  • The Target has disadvantage on saving throws against your combat maneuvers (shoving, grappling, etc.)
  • Killshot damage deals an extra PB d6 (e.g. PB +2 = 2d6)

Level 18 — Unbroken Resolve

When your Target reduces you to 0 HP, you instead drop to 1 HP. (Once per long rest.)
