Bandit

Level 3 — Dirty Fighting

You fight to win, not impress.

Cheap Shot

If you have advantage on an attack roll against your Target and hit, roll an additional weapon damage die.

Kick Dirt

As a bonus action, you can impose disadvantage on your Target's next attack against someone other than you (Uses: PB per long rest).

Level 10 — Opportunist's Edge

You excel at exploiting the chaos of battle.

Whenever a creature within 30 feet of you is hit by an ally's attack, you gain +2 to the next attack roll you make before the end of your next turn.

If that creature is your Target, you also deal +PB damage on that next hit.

.

Level 18 — Nothing Is Off the Table

You unleash your most vicious techniques.

Exposed Throat

When you hit your Target with a Signature Weapon attack while you have advantage, you may reroll all damage dice and use the higher total.

Cut the Escape

Your Target cannot take the Disengage or Dash actions while within 20 feet of you.

Cornered Finisher

If your Target is below 25% HP, your attacks against them automatically have advantage.
