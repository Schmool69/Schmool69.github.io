Brute Strain

Level 3 — Hulking Mutation

You count as one size larger when determining carrying capacity, grapples, and shoves.

Once per turn, when you hit a creature with a melee attack, you may deal extra damage equal to your Constitution modifier.

Visible change: Enlarged frame, bone growth, asymmetry

Level 6 Crushing Presence

When you move at least 10 ft toward a creature and hit it with a melee attack, it must succeed on a Strength save or be knocked prone.

If the creature is Large or smaller, you may also grapple it as part of the same attack.

Level 10 Reinforced Physiology

You gain resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing (choose one when you finish a long rest).

When you activate Adaptive Surge, you gain resistance to one additional damage type of your choice until the end of your next turn.

Level 14 Titanic Threat

Creatures of your size or smaller have disadvantage on checks made to escape your grapple.

When a creature you are grappling takes damage from you, it has disadvantage on its next attack roll.
