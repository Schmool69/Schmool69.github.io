Aberrant Strain

New Condition: Unnerved

A creature that is unnerved suffers the following effects:

  • It has disadvantage on Wisdom saving throws
  • If the creature is frightened, it also:
    • Cannot take reactions
    • Treats all creatures as lightly obscured beyond 10 ft (tunnel vision, stress)

The Unnerved condition:

  • Lasts for the duration specified by the effect that caused it
  • Does not stack with itself

Level 3 Visceral Wrongness

Your presence triggers an involuntary fear response in living creatures.

When you hit a creature with a melee attack, you may force it to make a Wisdom saving throw (DC = 8 + Proficiency Bonus + Constitution modifier).

On a failure, the creature becomes unnerved until the end of your next turn. If the creature was already unnerved, it instead becomes frightened of you until the end of your next turn.

You may only apply this feature once per turn.

Visible Change: Eyes reflecting light incorrectly, breath that doesn't match exertion, skin pulling tight in unnatural patterns.

Level 6 Panic Cascade

When a creature that is unnerved or frightened misses you with an attack, you may use your reaction to force it to make a Wisdom saving throw.

On a failure:

  • If it is unnerved, it becomes frightened of you until the end of its next turn
  • If it is frightened, it is also restrained until the start of its next turn

Level 10 Sustained Dread

When a creature becomes frightened of you due to one of your Aberrant Strain features, the condition lasts for one round as normal.

However, if the frightened condition would end when the creature is within 15 ft of you, the frightened condition instead continues.

The frightened condition ends only when the creature:

  • Ends its turn more than 15 ft away from you, or you are incapacitated
  • This does not maintain the restrained effect from panic cascade

Level 14 Unbearable Reality

At the start of a creature's turn within 10 ft of you:

  • If it is unnerved, it must succeed on a Wisdom save or become frightened
  • If it is frightened, it takes psychic damage equal to your Constitution modifier and has disadvantage on its next saving throw this turn
