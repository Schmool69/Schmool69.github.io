Swarm Strain

Level 3 Living Colony

You are surrounded by a Swarm Aura extending 5 ft from you.

When a creature starts its turn in your Swarm Aura, you may choose one:

  • It takes piercing damage equal to your Constitution modifier
  • Its speed is reduced by 10 ft until the end of its turn

You can affect a number of creatures per round equal to your Constitution modifier (minimum 1).

Visible Mutation: Crawling movement under the skin, chittering sounds, vents, writhing growths.

Level 6 Defensive Dispersal

When you take damage from a creature within 10 ft, you may use your reaction to:

  • Deal damage to that creature equal to your Constitution modifier
  • And impose disadvantage on its next attack roll before the end of its next turn

Additionally:

  • You have advantage on saving throws against being grappled or restrained

Level 10 Expanding Infestation

Your Swarm Aura increases to 10 ft.

Creatures affected by your Living Colony feature have disadvantage on Constitution saving throws until the start of their next turn.

When a creature within your aura is reduced to 0 HP, you gain temporary HP equal to your Constitution modifier.

Level 14 Devouring Host

At the start of your turn, choose any number of creatures within your Swarm Aura.

Each must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or suffer one of the following effects (your choice per creature):

  • Be poisoned until the start of your next turn
  • Have its speed reduced to 0 until the start of your next turn
  • Take damage equal to twice your Constitution modifier

Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.
