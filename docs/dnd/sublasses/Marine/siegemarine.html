Siege Marine

Level 3 Anchored Fire

If you move no more than 5 feet on your turn, you gain advantage on your first weapon attack that turn.

Additionally, your Suppressive Fire affects all hostile creatures of your choice within 10 feet of the original target.

Level 7 Iron Position

While you are not incapacitated:

  • You have advantage on saving throws against being moved, knocked prone, or disarmed
  • Allies within 5 feet of you gain half cover

Level 10 Kill Zone

Creatures that start their turn within 10 feet of you have their speed reduced by 10 feet.

Additionally, when a creature affected by your Suppressive Fire takes damage, it takes additional damage equal to your proficiency bonus the first time each turn.

Level 14 Unbreakable Line

When you would be forced to move or knocked prone, you may instead choose not to move.

Once per long rest, when you drop to 0 hit points, you may immediately make one weapon attack before falling unconscious.

Level 18 Walking Bastion

You are immune to being frightened, charmed or stunned.

Additionally, once per turn when a creature fails its saving throw against your Suppressive Fire, you may immediately make a weapon attack against that creature as part of the same reaction.
