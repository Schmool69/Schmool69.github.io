Infantry Gunner

Level 3 Sustained Fire

Once per turn when you hit a creature with a ranged weapon attack, you may mark the target until the start of your next turn.

The marked creature takes additional damage equal to your proficiency bonus the first time you hit it each turn and its speed is reduced by 10 feet until the end of that turn.

Level 7 Suppression Specialist

Your mastery of ranged fire enhances your core features:

  • When a creature fails its saving throw against your Suppressive Fire caused by a ranged weapon attack, it also has disadvantage on Dexterity saving throws until the start of its next turn
  • Creatures affected by your Suppressive Fire cannot take the Dash action

Level 10 Kill Rhythm

When you hit a creature with a ranged weapon attack on consecutive turns, the second hit deals additional damage equal to twice your proficiency bonus.

This bonus resets if you miss or change targets before your last target dies.

Level 14 Fire Superiority

You gain the following benefits while wielding a ranged weapon:

  • You ignore half cover
  • When a creature you can see within your weapon's normal range attacks an ally, you may use your reaction to impose disadvantage on that attack roll

Level 18 Force of Attrition

Once per turn, when you deal damage to a creature with a ranged weapon attack, you may choose one:

  • Reduce the target's speed to 0 until the start of its next turn
  • Prevent the target from taking actions this turn

If the creature is affected by your Suppressive Fire, you may apply both effects instead.
