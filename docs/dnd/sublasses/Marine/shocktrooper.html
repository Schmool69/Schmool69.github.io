Shock Trooper

Level 3 Breach Momentum

When you move at least 10 feet straight toward a hostile creature and hit it with a melee attack:

  • You deal extra damage equal to your proficiency bonus
  • The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be pushed 5 feet or knocked prone (your choice)

Level 7 Close-Quarters Terror

Creatures you hit with melee attacks have disadvantage on their next attack roll before the end of their next turn.

Additionally, hostile creatures within 5 feet of you have disadvantage on saving throws against your Suppressive Fire.

Level 10 Bodycam Off

While you are within 5 feet of a hostile creature:

  • Your melee weapon attacks score a critical hit on a roll of 19-20
  • When you hit a creature that is prone, grappled, or restrained, you deal additional damage equal to your proficiency bonus

Level 14 Shockwave Entry

When you use military grit, you may immediately move up to your speed without provoking opportunity attacks. At the end of this movement, choose any number of hostile creatures within 5 feet, each must make a Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

Level 18 Perimeter Sweep

Once per turn, when you reduce a creature to 0 hit points with a melee attack, you may immediately force another hostile creature within 10 feet to make a Wisdom saving throw.

On a failure, the creature is frightened of you until the end of its next turn. While frightened this way, it also has disadvantage on its next saving throw against your Suppressive Fire.
