Dead Man's Hand

Level 3 Last Chip

When you are below half HP:

  • Gain advantage on your first attack each turn
  • Your Odds Dice deal +2 damage when added to attacks

Level 6 One in the Chamber

When you make an attack roll, you may declare One in the Chamber before rolling.

Anything above 1:

  • The attack has advantage
  • If it hits, it deals +2d6 damage

On a 1:

  • The attack automatically misses
  • You take damage equal to your Gambler level
  • You cannot use Odds Dice until the end of your next turn

Level 10 Dead Man Walking

Once per long rest, when reduced to 0 HP:

  • Drop to 1 HP instead
  • Immediately take a turn

Level 14 No Fold

While below half HP:

  • The first time each turn you take damage, reduce it by 1d6
  • Enemies have disadvantage on opportunity attacks against you
