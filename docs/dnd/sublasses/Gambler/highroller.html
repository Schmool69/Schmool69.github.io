High Roller

Level 3 Set the Stakes

As a bonus action, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet and declare the stakes.

Choose one stake:

Reputation

The creature has disadvantage on its next roll against you. If it fails that roll, you gain advantage on all Charisma-based checks against it for 1 minute.

Control

The creature's speed is reduced by 10 feet until the end of its next turn. If it fails an attack roll during this time, it loses its reaction.

Pressure

The creature takes a 1 penalty to all d20 rolls until the end of its next turn. If it fails a roll, you gain advantage on your next roll against it.

Uses per long rest = Charisma modifier

Level 6 Table Presence

At the start of a social scene or combat:

Choose one creature you can see. It has disadvantage on its first roll involving you.

Level 10 Read the Crowd

When dealing with groups, you may treat a group as a single creature for Tell, House Edge, and any social checks (important with odds dice usage).

Level 14 Too Big to Fail

Once per long rest, when you would fail a Charisma-based check, you succeed instead.
