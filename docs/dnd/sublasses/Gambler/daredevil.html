Daredevil

Level 3 — Close Call

When a hostile creature misses you with an attack:

  • You may immediately move up to 10 feet without provoking opportunity attacks
  • If you end this movement closer to the attacker, you gain advantage on your next attack against it before the end of your next turn

Level 6 — Momentum Rush

Once per turn, if you moved at least 10 feet on your turn, gain one of the following until the start of your next turn:

  • Advantage on Dexterity saving throws
  • Advantage on your first attack roll
  • +1 to odds dice rolls (>5 = crit)

Level 10 — Skin of Your Teeth

Once per long rest, when you would fail any kind of attack saving throw, you instead succeed on the save.

Level 14 — No Looking Back

Once per turn when you use Dash, Disengage, or Dodge:

  • You may make one weapon attack as part of the same action
  • If the attack hits, you may immediately move up to 10 feet without provoking opportunity attacks
