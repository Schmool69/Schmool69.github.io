Cyber Ghost

"You can't hit what you can't detect."

A stealth-specialist hacker who manipulates light, sensors, and digital signatures.

Level 3 Ghost Protocol

You gain proficiency in Stealth. You can attempt to hide when lightly obscured by digital noise (sparks, holograms, screens, machinery).

Additionally, when you hit a creature with your Hack:

  • You may become digitally masked against it until the start of your next turn
  • While masked, that creature has disadvantage on Perception checks to detect you and cannot make opportunity attacks against you

Level 3 Cloaking Burst

As a bonus action, you activate a local cloaking loop:

  • You gain advantage on Stealth checks for 1 minute
  • Creatures have disadvantage to attack you if you have any cover
  • You can use this feature proficiency bonus times per long rest

Level 6 Phase Dash

You can briefly desync your physical position with reality.

When you move on your turn:

  • You do not provoke opportunity attacks
  • You can move through a creature's space (but cannot end there)

Once between long rests, you can also:

  • Move through solid objects for up to 10 feet (you must end in an open space)

Level 13 Optic Scrambler

When you hit a creature with your Hack, you can force it to make a Con save. On failure:

  • The creature is Blinded until the end of its next turn
  • Can be used Int mod times per long rest (minimum 1)

Level 18 Vanish.exe

As an action:

  • You turn invisible for 1 minute
  • This invisibility does not break when you attack
  • Sensors, heat detection, blindsight, and tremorsense cannot detect you
  • Only true sight works
  • Use once per long rest
