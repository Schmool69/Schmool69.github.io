Neural Breaker

"The mind runs on impulses. So do I."

A psychological and biological hacker who overloads neural systems.

Level 3 Neural Spike

When you hit a creature with your Hack, you may force it to make a Wisdom save. On a failure:

  • It has disadvantage on its next attack roll
  • You may use this feature Int mod times per long rest

Level 3 Bio-Scanner

You automatically know:

  • A creature's current HP (exact)
  • Any biological weaknesses
  • Any condition immunities

Analyze as a bonus action.

Level 6 Synapse Crash

After you hit with a Hack, you may trigger a neural overload.

The target makes a Con save. On failure:

  • It is Stunned until the end of your next turn

Usable once per short rest. (Strong effect but tightly limited.)

Level 13 Pain Loop Injection

When a creature within 30 feet damages you, you can use your reaction to attack its pain receptors.

The creature makes a Wisdom save. On failure:

  • It takes 3d8 psychic/system hybrid damage
  • It has disadvantage on all attacks until the end of its next turn

Usable proficiency bonus times per long rest.

Level 18 Total Neural Hijack

As an action, choose one creature with a nervous system within 30 feet.

It must make an Int save. On a failed save:

  • You control it like a Dominate Monster effect for 1 minute
  • No concentration required
  • Creature treats you as a trusted ally
  • One use per long rest
