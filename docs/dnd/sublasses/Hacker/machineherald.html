Machine Herald

"Why fight the enemy when you can turn their machines into yours?"

Drone specialist, battlefield controller, technomancer without magic.

Level 3 Combat Drone

You build a small autonomous drone.

Stats:

  • AC 15
  • HP = twice your Hacker level
  • Speed 30 ft, fly 20 ft
  • Immune to poison & psychic

It acts on your initiative and requires no commands (but only takes Dodge unless you use your bonus action to issue a command).

Commands (bonus action):

  • Strike: Ranged attack + INT mod; 1d6 tech damage
  • Scan: Give an ally advantage on their next attack vs a target
  • Shield: Give an ally within 5 ft +2 AC until your next turn

If destroyed, you can rebuild it with 1 hour of work.

Level 6 Dual-Link Interface

Your drone can now take two actions when you command it:

  • Strike twice
  • Strike + Scan
  • Scan + Shield
  • Etc.

Level 13 Override Subsystems

You can attempt to control enemy robots/constructs.

As an action, choose a construct/robot within 60 ft. It must make an Int save. On failure:

  • You control it for 1 minute, as if it were your drone (bonus action commands)
  • Once you use this, you can't use it again until a long rest

Level 18 Swarm Fabricator

You can deploy two drones at once.

  • Each drone acts independently
  • Your bonus action can command both simultaneously
  • Drones can combine their Scans to give an ally advantage +1d6 on their next attack
  • Drones can Shield the same ally for +4 AC
  • If one drone is destroyed, you can rebuild it in 10 minutes
