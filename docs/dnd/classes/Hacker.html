Hacker

Hackers are brilliant technologists who weaponize code and systems. They manipulate technology with surgical precision to accomplish tasks both in the field and behind the scenes.

Class Features

Hit Points

Hit Die: 1d6

HP at 1st Level: 6 + Con mod

HP per level: 1d6 + Con mod

Armor & Weapon Proficiencies

Armor: Light armor

Weapons: Simple weapons

Tools: Proficiency with Hacking Rig

Saving Throws

Intelligence, Dexterity

Skills

Choose 2 from: Investigation, Perception, Insight, Stealth, Sleight of Hand, History

Level 1 — Hack

  • Ranged attack, 120 ft
  • 1d10 tech damage
  • Uses INT for attack and damage
  • Beams scale at 5/11/17

Level 1 — Hacking Rig

Your tool for Hacks. Functions like an arcane focus but with tech flavor.

Level 1 — Tech Savant

  • Proficiency in Technology checks
  • Add double proficiency to Technology checks made to analyze or infiltrate electronic systems (Equivalent to Expertise)

Level 2 — Hack Modules

You gain 2 modules, gaining more over time (up to 10). See Hack Modules below.

Level 3 — Specialization

Choose a subclass:

  • Cyber Ghost - stealthy infiltrator
  • Machine Herald - drone & bot controller
  • Neural Breaker - mental disruptor / anti-biological expert

Level 5 — Improved Rig Optimization

Choose one:

  • Overclocked Hacking: Add your Intelligence modifier to the damage of each Hack beam that hits.
  • Rapid Injection Protocol: Once per turn, when you hit a creature with a Hack beam, you may apply a second Hack Module to that hit without using your per-turn module limit.
  • Combat Auto-Targeting: You gain a +1 bonus to ranged attack rolls, including Hacks. Your Hack range increases to 180 ft.

Level 7 — Adaptive Assault

Once per turn when you make a Hack, you may reroll one beam's attack roll. You must use the new roll.

Level 9 — Advanced Hack Routine

You may apply two different Hack Modules to the same Hack once per turn.

Level 10 — Expert Diagnostics

You gain permanent passive analysis buffs:

  • You cannot have disadvantage on Technology checks
  • As a bonus action, you can scan a target and learn the following stats: AC, Current HP, Resistances/immunities

Level 14 — Adaptive Algorithms

Whenever you miss all beams of your Hack on your turn, you may:

  • Reroll one of those beams as part of the same action
  • OR gain +2 AC until the start of your next turn

Level 15 — Superior Hack Routine

Once per short rest, you may choose to automatically hit with one beam of Hack.

Level 20 — Master Hacker

Once per turn, you may:

  • Apply any Hack Module you know to a Hack without it counting toward your "once per turn" limit
  • When you roll damage for Hack, treat any 1s or 2s as 3s instead

Hack Modules — Set of 10

1. System Lag

When you hit a creature with your Hack, you may reduce its speed by 10 feet until the start of your next turn. A creature can only be affected once per turn.

2. Overclock Burst

Once per turn when you hit a creature with Hack, you deal extra tech damage equal to your Intelligence modifier.

3. Sensor Jam

When you hit a creature with your Hack, it has disadvantage on the next attack roll it makes before the end of its next turn.

4. Neural Backdoor

When you hit a creature with your Hack, you plant a temporary backdoor in its systems. Until the end of your next turn:

  • You have advantage on your next Hack roll made against that creature
  • If that next Hack hits, it deals extra system disruption damage equal to your proficiency bonus

You can only maintain a Neural Backdoor on one creature at a time.

5. Directive Injection

When you hit a creature with Hack, you may mark it with a Directive until the end of your next turn. The next ally who attacks that target gains +2 to the attack roll.

6. Overload Pulse

Once per turn, when you hit a creature with Hack, you can force it to make a Strength saving throw. On failure, the creature is pushed 10 feet directly away from you.

7. Firewall Breach

Your Hacks ignore resistance to their damage type. Additionally, when you hit a creature with Hack, it makes a Dexterity saving throw. On a failure, its AC is reduced by 1 until the start of your next turn (stacking with itself only once).

8. Packet Storm

When you take the Attack action to use Hack, you may target an additional creature within 10 feet of the original target. The secondary target takes half your Hack damage on a hit (no riders like Sensor Jam, Slow, etc.).

9. Reboot Pulse

When you reduce a creature or device to 0 hit points with Hack, you may cause another creature of your choice within 30 feet to gain temporary hit points equal to your Intelligence modifier + proficiency bonus.

10. Ghost Intrusion

When you hit a creature with your Hack, you can become digitally masked against it until the start of your next turn. You gain:

  • +5 to Stealth checks against that creature
  • It has disadvantage on Perception checks to detect you
  • If you move, you do not provoke opportunity attacks from that creature
