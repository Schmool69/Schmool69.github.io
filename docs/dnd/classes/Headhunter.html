Headhunter

Headhunters are elite assassins and trackers with unparalleled focus and precision. They lock onto their prey and eliminate them with calculated brutality.

Class Features

Hit Points

Hit Die: 1d8

HP at 1st Level: 8 + Con mod

HP per level: 1d8 + Con mod

Armor & Weapon Proficiencies

Armor: Light armor

Weapons: Simple and martial weapons

Tools: Proficiency with infiltration toolkit (thieves' tools)

Saving Throws

Choose 2 from: Wisdom, Dexterity, Charisma

Skills

Choose 3 from: Acrobatics, Athletics, Insight, Intimidation, Investigation, Perception, Stealth, Sleight of Hand, Survival

Level 1 Target Acquisition

At the start of each combat, a random hostile creature is selected for you as your Target for this encounter.

When attacking your Target, you gain:

  • +2 to attack rolls
  • Bonus damage once per turn: Starts at +1d6 at Level 1, scales up to +10d6 by high levels (called 'Killshot')
  • Advantage on checks to track, pursue, or study them
  • Advantage when attempting to detect their location (sound, footprints, etc.)

Re-Selecting a Target: If the Target is downed or becomes unreachable, you may spend a bonus action to mark a new Target.

Level 1 Signature Weapon

At 1st level, you designate one weapon as your Signature Weapon. You gain the following benefits:

Universal Signature Weapon Rules:

  • You gain proficiency with this weapon if you didn't already have it
  • You may use Dexterity or Strength for attack and damage rolls with this weapon (your choice for each attack)
  • You gain a +1 bonus to damage rolls made with your Signature Weapon
  • If it's lost or destroyed, you can replace or recalibrate it during a long rest

Melee Signature Weapon Benefits:

  • When you hit a creature with a melee attack using your Signature Weapon, you may deal extra damage equal to your proficiency bonus (once per turn)
  • If your Target is within your reach, you gain either: +1 AC until the start of your next turn, OR +5 ft. movement immediately after the attack

Ranged Signature Weapon Benefits:

  • Long-range attacks with your Signature Weapon do not impose disadvantage (you still can't exceed maximum weapon range)
  • Once per turn, when you attack your Target with your Signature Weapon, you gain a +1 bonus to the attack roll

Level 2 Tactical Maneuver

As a bonus action, choose one of the following options:

Pursue: Move up to 10 feet directly toward your Target. This movement does not provoke opportunity attacks.

Withdraw: If your Target is within 10 feet of you, you may move up to 10 feet directly away from them without provoking opportunity attacks.

Ambush Angle: If you have not moved yet on this turn, you may gain advantage on the next attack roll you make against your Target before the end of the turn.

Level 3 Hunter's Instincts

You gain proficiency in Survival or Insight (player choice) and expertise in one of the following: Perception, Stealth, Investigation, or Insight.

Level 3 Honorific (Subclass)

Choose a subclass that reflects your hunting philosophy.

Level 5 Bullet Time

When your Target hits you with an attack, you can use your reaction to force them to reroll the attack. They must use the new roll. You can use this a number of times equal to your Dexterity modifier before refreshing with a long rest.

Level 6 Signature Weapon Upgrade I

Melee Options:

  • Armor Shear: Melee attacks with the Signature Weapon ignore resistance to slashing, piercing, or bludgeoning damage
  • Strong Stance: When you hit your Target with a melee Signature Weapon attack, you gain advantage on your next Strength or Dexterity saving throw until the start of your next turn

Ranged Options:

  • Frag Bullets: When you hit your Target with a ranged signature weapon attack, you deal 1d6 additional weapon damage if the target moves at least 10 feet that turn
  • Thermal Sight: You can see your Target through non-total obscurement (fog, dim-light, smoke)

Level 7 Pressure Point Analysis

Once per turn, when you deal Killshot damage to your Target, you may impose one of the following effects on the Target:

  • Hobble: The Target's speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn
  • Distract: The Target has disadvantage on its next attack roll before the start of your next turn
  • Expose: Until the end of your next turn, the Target cannot benefit from half cover against you

Level 9 Efficient Recovery

Once per long rest, when you finish a short rest, you may restore yourself to full hit points (instead of using hit dice).

Level 11 Chain Designation

You can maintain up to two Targets at the same time. All passive Target-based class features apply to both Targets. When an ability refers to "your Target," you may choose either Target at the moment of resolution. You still only deal Killshot damage once per turn, even with multiple Targets marked.

Level 13 Signature Weapon Upgrade II

Melee Options:

  • Overdrive Strike: Once per turn, when you hit your Target with a melee Signature Weapon attack, you deal extra damage equal to your proficiency bonus (in addition to your existing bonus from the Level 1 melee mode)
  • Oppress: The Target's speed is reduced by 10 feet, and the Target has disadvantage on its next attack roll (if it occurs before your next turn)

Ranged Options:

  • Phantom Shot: Once per turn, if you hit your Target with your ranged Signature Weapon while hidden, the attack does not reveal your position
  • Concentrate: If you haven't moved on your turn, your first attack against your Target deals extra Killshot damage equal to your proficiency bonus

Level 14 Adaptive Focus

You gain complete control over your Target system.

Choose Your Target: As a bonus action, you may select any hostile creature you can see within 120 feet to become your Target. This bypasses the usual random assignment.

Lock-On Stability: Once per turn when you attack your Target and miss, you gain a +2 bonus to the next attack roll you make against that same Target before the end of your next turn.

Level 15 Evanesce

Your disappearance techniques evolve into a reliable cloaking method. You may activate Evanesce when any of the following occur: You reduce a creature to 0 hit points or you take the Hide action.

When you activate Evanesce, you gain:

  • You become hidden if there is any concealment or cover
  • If no cover is present, you instead gain heavily obscured status against your Target until the start of your next turn, treating yourself as effectively invisible to them

This feature may be used a number of times equal to your Dexterity modifier, refreshing on a long rest.

Level 17 Apex Predator

Your Killshot becomes devastatingly efficient. When you deal Killshot damage to your Target:

  • You add your Dexterity modifier to the Killshot damage (again). This stacks with normal damage modifiers.
  • If your Target has no allies within 30 feet, double the Killshot damage dice.

Level 20 Perfect Assassin

Against your Target, you gain:

  • Expanded Critical Range: Your Signature Weapon attacks crit on a 1920
  • Dead to Rights: Once per short rest, when you hit your Target, you may treat the attack roll as a critical hit instead
  • Unshakeable Designation: Your Target cannot gain invisibility, concealment, or disguise against you
