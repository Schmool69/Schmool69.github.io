Gambler

Gamblers are cunning risk-takers who thrive on chance and probability. They read people and situations with uncanny precision, turning luck itself into a weapon.

Class Features

Hit Points

Hit Die: 1d8

HP at 1st Level: 8 + Con mod

HP per level: 1d8 + Con mod

Armor & Weapon Proficiencies

Armor: Light armor

Weapons: Simple weapons, One Finesse weapon of choice

Tools: Proficiency with Gaming sets

Saving Throws

Charisma, Wisdom

Skills

Choose 3 from: Deception, Persuasion, Insight, Sleight of Hand, Perception, Investigation, Performance

Level 1 — Odds Dice

Unless otherwise stated, you may spend 1 Odds Die after rolling a d20 but before the result is declared to roll a d6 and add it to the roll.

  • If the result is a critical fail, a negative effect will occur (at DM discretion)
  • If the result is a critical success, a positive effect will occur (at DM discretion)

Number of odds dice per level is in class table and they refresh on long rest

Level 1 — Read the Table

You gain advantage on Insight checks to detect lies, bluffs, or intent and checks involving games of chance or wagers.

Level 2 — Tell

When a creature you can see makes a d20 roll, you may use your reaction to mark that creature's Tell until the end of your next turn.

While a creature has a Tell, you gain advantage on your next attack roll against it or Deception, Persuasion, or Insight check involving it.

You may use this feature a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier per long rest.

Level 3 — Calculated Risk

Once per turn, when you spend an Odds Die, you may choose to increase the stakes:

  • On roll success: add +1d6 damage or impose disadvantage on the target's next roll
  • On roll failure: disadvantage on your next roll

Level 3 — Gambler Archetype

Choose a subclass:

  • High Roller - social-based party face
  • Dead Man's Hand - high-risk low-hp combat
  • Daredevil - High-speed intense combat

Level 5 — Double or Nothing

When you spend an Odds Die, you may spend one additional Odds Die:

  • Add both dice to the roll
  • Amplify the crit success/failure effects (occurring at above 10 or below 4)

Level 7 — Shift the Odds

When a creature within 30ft makes a roll, use your reaction to spend 1 odds die and add or subtract the die from the roll.

Level 9 — Luck Turns

  • When you roll a natural 1: You may immediately regain 1 spent Odds Die
  • When you roll a natural 20: Your next Odds Die roll automatically counts as a 6

Level 11 — House Edge

Once per round, when a creature you can see within 30 feet fails an attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you may choose one:

  • Gain advantage on your next roll against that creature before the end of your next turn
  • Immediately move up to 10 feet without provoking opportunity attacks

Level 13 — Press Your Advantage

Once per turn, when you have advantage on a roll and hit or succeed, you may deal +1d6 damage or impose disadvantage on the target's next roll.

Level 15 — The Long Shot

Once per long rest, before making a roll, declare a Long Shot and roll three Odds Dice, adding them to the roll.

Level 17 — Loaded Dice

Once per long rest, when you roll an Odds Die, you may treat the result as a 6.

Level 18 — Luck Runs Out

When a hostile creature critically succeeds or fails, you may spend 1 Odds Die to invert the outcome (crit → normal, fail → normal). Once per short rest.

Level 20 — All In

Once per long rest, you may spend any number of Odds Dice on a single roll. Each Odds Die that rolls 4+ counts as a 6.
